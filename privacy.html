Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

FlowSync System (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under applicable data protection laws including GDPR.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Identity data: name, email, phone number.
  • Social media enquiry data: messages sent through Instagram, WhatsApp, website forms.
  • Booking data: information submitted through Calendly when scheduling calls.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, analytics.
  • Payment data: handled securely by Stripe — we never store card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and messages
  • Provide our automation and booking services
  • Improve our website, tools, and user experience
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send service updates or relevant notifications

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent — when you contact us or submit a form
  • Contract — when you purchase or use our service
  • Legitimate interest — improving services, preventing fraud

4. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell or distribute your data. We may share information only with trusted service providers such as:

  • Instagram / Meta
  • WhatsApp
  • Calendly (booking management)
  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for legal, contractual, or operational reasons.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to certain types of data processing

8. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information.
