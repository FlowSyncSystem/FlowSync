Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

FlowSync System (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under applicable data protection laws including the GDPR.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity data: name, email, phone number.
  • Social media enquiry data: messages sent through Instagram, WhatsApp, website forms.
  • Booking data: information submitted via Calendly when scheduling calls.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, analytics.
  • Payment data: securely handled by Stripe — we do not store card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries.
  • To schedule and manage appointments.
  • To operate FlowSync automation services.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We may share data with trusted processors such as: Stripe, Calendly, Google Analytics, and social media messaging APIs.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data.
