Frequently Asked Questions

How does FlowSync handle my calls? +
FlowSync uses advanced conversational AI to answer your business line instantly, book appointments natively into your calendar, and handle common customer inquiries just like a human receptionist would.
Does it sound like a robot? +
Not at all. We utilize the latest voice generation models to ensure latency is practically zero and the tone is professional, warm, and highly human-like.
Does it connect with my existing calendar? +
Yes. FlowSync integrates seamlessly with Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly, and most major CRM platforms to read your availability and push new appointments directly.
Can I customize the AI's instructions? +
Absolutely. You provide the core business context, FAQs, and rules for booking. The AI strictly adheres to your custom operational guardrails.
What happens if a call is too complex for the AI? +
If a caller has a highly specific issue outside the AI's knowledge base, FlowSync will politely take a message, collect their contact info, and instantly forward a transcript to your team.
